Ramen on a budget.

Ingredients

  • Whole Fish, still scaled
  • Maruchan Ramen Packet Chicken Flavored
  • Frozen Spinach
  • Block of Mozerella cheese
  • Browncap Mushrooms
  • Water

Steps

  1. Start boiling your water
  2. Open your ramen packet, let it air out at room temperature, then throw it in boiling water
  3. Get your fish, steam it by any method of your choice
  4. Cut your block of cheese in half
  5. Open frozen spinach, let sit for 90 days
  6. Mix together everything except the cheese while still cooking, let sit and simmer
  7. Don't bother cooking your mushrooms, just throw them in at the end
  8. Enjoy!
